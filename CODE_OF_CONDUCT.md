# Code Of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want MCP Bridge to be a welcoming, respectful project for everyone.

Participants are expected to:

- Be kind and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Offer feedback on ideas without attacking people
- Respect different backgrounds and levels of experience

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or intimidation
- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith engagement

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Scope

This applies within project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community channels related to the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately through the process described in [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) or by contacting the maintainers.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will review reports and decide on an appropriate response, including warning, moderation, removal, or ban when necessary.
